Facts About Cats

Basic Info

Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world and have lived with humans for about 9,000 years. They are small,carnivorous mammels that are often kept as pets.Cats are skilled hunters with sharp claws and retractable paws to help them climb and catch prey they communicate through vocalization, body languages and leaving scents. There known for their agility, Independance, playfulness and curiosity.Cats have excellent night vision and can see in light levels six times lower then what humans need cats sleep with an average of 13 to 16 hours a day so they have energy for short times of play.
